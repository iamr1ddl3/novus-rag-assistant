# EMI and Repayment Policy — Novus Bank

## EMI Setup
- All Novus Bank loans (personal, home, auto) use NACH (National Automated Clearing House) mandate for EMI auto-debit.
- Mandate registered at loan origination; customer signs digitally via Aadhaar OTP.
- EMI deduction date choices: **5th, 10th, or 15th** of each month (selected at application; cannot be changed after first disbursement).
- First EMI due: one full calendar month after disbursement date.

## What Happens on EMI Due Date
1. Auto-debit attempted from designated account at 9 AM.
2. If successful: payment posted; SMS confirmation sent.
3. If insufficient funds: retry attempted on next 2 working days (9 AM each day).
4. If all 3 attempts fail: EMI marked as bounced on the 3rd working day.

## Bounce Charges
- ₹500 per bounce (flat) + applicable GST, debited from the account on the day of bounce confirmation.
- If account balance is insufficient for bounce charge too, the charge is added to loan outstanding and attracts loan interest.
- Three bounces in a rolling 12-month period triggers a credit review and may result in loan recall.

## Grace Period
- 3 calendar days after EMI due date before the payment is marked as overdue.
- Example: EMI due on 10th → overdue status applied from 14th if not paid.
- Grace period does not waive bounce charges; it only affects overdue classification.

## Late Payment Interest
- 2% per month on the overdue EMI amount (calculated from the original due date, not the bounce date).
- Charged pro-rata for partial months.
- Late interest is separate from bounce charges; both may apply simultaneously.

## Partial Prepayment
- Minimum prepayment amount: ₹10,000 per transaction.
- Allowed after: 6 EMIs for personal loans; 12 EMIs for home loans; 3 EMIs for auto loans.
- Fee: 2% on prepaid amount for personal loans; 0% for floating-rate home loans; 2% for fixed-rate home loans during lock-in.
- After prepayment: customer can choose to (a) reduce EMI (same tenure) or (b) reduce tenure (same EMI). Default is (b).
- Prepayment credited to principal; does not offset upcoming EMI.

## Full Foreclosure
- Personal loans: allowed after 12 EMIs. Charge: 1% of outstanding principal.
- Home loans (floating): allowed anytime after 12 EMIs. Zero charge.
- Home loans (fixed, lock-in): 2% of outstanding principal during lock-in period; zero after.
- Elite customers (personal loan): zero foreclosure charges after 18 EMIs.
- Foreclosure settlement: pay outstanding principal + accrued interest + charges via NEFT/RTGS. Do not use UPI for amounts > ₹1 lakh.

## Moratorium
- Available only during periods declared by RBI or Government of India as eligible for moratorium relief.
- Not available on-demand; Novus Bank cannot grant moratorium independently.
- During moratorium: EMI not deducted; interest continues to accrue and is added to principal (compounding).
- Bounce charges waived during official moratorium periods.

## Loan Restructuring
- Available for borrowers facing genuine financial hardship (job loss, medical emergency, natural disaster).
- Application: written request to Novus Bank with supporting documents (termination letter, medical bills, etc.).
- Review timeline: 15 working days from submission of complete documents.
- Restructuring options: EMI reduction (tenure extension), payment holiday (3–6 months), or hybrid.
- Restructuring is reported to credit bureaus; may impact CIBIL score.
- Maximum restructuring allowed: once per loan.

## Payment Methods for Manual EMI/Prepayment
- NEFT/RTGS to loan account number (IFSC: NOVS0001XXX).
- Novus Bank app → Loans → Pay EMI.
- **Not accepted:** Cash deposits, cheques, UPI for amounts > ₹1 lakh.
