# Fees and Charges Schedule — Novus Bank

## Account Maintenance Charges

| Charge Type | Standard | Novus Plus | Novus Elite |
|-------------|----------|------------|-------------|
| Annual maintenance fee | ₹299/year | ₹999/year | ₹2,999/year |
| Non-maintenance of AQB | ₹500/quarter | ₹750/quarter | Not applicable |
| Account reactivation (dormant) | ₹500 + KYC | ₹250 + KYC | Free |
| Cheque book (25 leaves) | ₹100 | ₹50 | Free |
| Demand draft issuance | ₹75 | ₹50 | Free |
| Stop payment instruction | ₹100 | ₹50 | Free |

**Note:** Annual maintenance fee is charged on April 1 each year. Prorated for accounts opened mid-year.

## ATM Charges

### Novus Bank ATMs (Own Network)
- **All tiers:** Unlimited free transactions (cash withdrawal and balance enquiry).

### Other Bank ATMs (Domestic)
| Tier | Free Transactions/Month | Charge After Free Limit |
|------|------------------------|------------------------|
| Standard | 3 per month | ₹20 per transaction |
| Novus Plus | 5 per month | ₹15 per transaction |
| Novus Elite | Unlimited | Free |

- Balance enquiry at other bank ATMs: ₹10 per enquiry after free limit (Standard); free for Plus/Elite.
- Failed ATM transaction (funds not dispensed but deducted): No charge; reversal within 5 working days.

### International ATM Transactions
- Cash withdrawal: ₹150 per transaction + 3.5% foreign currency conversion markup.
- Balance enquiry: ₹25 per enquiry.
- Plus/Elite customers: ₹100 per withdrawal + 2.5% markup.
- **International ATM usage must be enabled in app before travel** (disabled by default).

## GST on Bank Charges
- GST at 18% applicable on all fee-based services listed above.
- GST is included in the charges listed where marked; where not specified, add 18%.
- GST exempt: Interest income, insurance premiums, NRI remittances.

## Charges Revision Policy
- Novus Bank may revise charges with **30 days' prior notice** via app notification and registered email.
- Revised schedule published on novusbank.in/fees before effective date.
- Customers who close account within 30 days of fee revision notice: no exit penalty.
