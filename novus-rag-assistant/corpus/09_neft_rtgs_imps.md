# NEFT, RTGS, and IMPS Policy — Novus Bank

## Overview
Novus Bank supports three interbank transfer modes: NEFT (batch-based), RTGS (high-value real-time), and IMPS (instant 24/7). All three are available via the Novus app, net banking, and branch.

## NEFT (National Electronic Funds Transfer)

### Availability
- Monday to Saturday: 8:00 AM – 7:00 PM (IST).
- Excluding public holidays declared by RBI.
- 48 half-hourly settlement batches per day.

### Limits
- Minimum: ₹1.
- Maximum: No upper limit (but transactions above ₹2 lakh are recommended via RTGS for same-day settlement).

### Charges (per transaction)

| Amount Slab | Standard Customer | Novus Plus | Novus Elite |
|-------------|-------------------|------------|-------------|
| Up to ₹10,000 | ₹2.50 | Free (first 20/month) | Free (unlimited) |
| ₹10,001 – ₹1,00,000 | ₹5.00 | Free (first 20/month) | Free (unlimited) |
| ₹1,00,001 – ₹2,00,000 | ₹15.00 | Free (first 20/month) | Free (unlimited) |
| Above ₹2,00,000 | ₹25.00 | Free (first 20/month) | Free (unlimited) |

### Settlement Timelines
- Funds credited within the same half-hourly batch if initiated before the batch cut-off.
- Example: Transfer initiated at 10:15 AM → credited in the 10:30 AM batch → beneficiary receives funds by 10:45 AM typically.
- Late-day transfers (after 6:30 PM on weekdays) are processed in the first batch of the next working day.

### Failed NEFT
- If beneficiary account is closed/incorrect: amount returned to sender within 2 hours of the failed batch.
- NEFT cannot be cancelled once submitted.

## RTGS (Real Time Gross Settlement)

### Availability
- Monday to Friday: 8:00 AM – 4:30 PM (IST).
- Saturday: 8:00 AM – 2:00 PM.
- Not available on Sundays and public holidays.

### Limits
- **Minimum: ₹2,00,000.** (RTGS is only for high-value transfers.)
- Maximum: No upper limit.

### Charges

| Amount Slab | All Customers |
|-------------|---------------|
| ₹2,00,000 – ₹5,00,000 | ₹25.00 |
| Above ₹5,00,000 | ₹50.00 |

- Elite customers: RTGS charges waived (unlimited free).

### Settlement Timeline
- Real-time: credited to beneficiary within 30 minutes (usually within 5 minutes during business hours).
- RTGS initiated after 4:30 PM on weekdays or 2:00 PM on Saturday: processed the next working day.

---

## IMPS (Immediate Payment Service)

### Availability
- **24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year** (including Sundays and public holidays).

### Limits
- Per transaction: ₹5,00,000.
- Daily: ₹5,00,000 (shared with UPI daily limit for outward transfers).

### Charges

| Amount | Charge |
|--------|--------|
| Up to ₹1,000 | ₹2.50 |
| ₹1,001 – ₹25,000 | ₹5.00 |
| ₹25,001 – ₹1,00,000 | ₹10.00 |
| ₹1,00,001 – ₹5,00,000 | ₹15.00 |

- Elite customers: IMPS charges waived.

### Settlement Timeline
- **Immediate:** Credited within seconds under normal conditions.
- Maximum 30 minutes in case of system delays.
- Failed IMPS: auto-reversal within 2 hours.

---

## Adding a Beneficiary
- New beneficiary: activation cooldown of **30 minutes** (security feature; cannot be waived).
- Transfer limit for new beneficiary in first 24 hours: ₹25,000 (all modes combined).
- After 24 hours: full limits apply.
- Verified beneficiaries (added > 30 days ago with successful transfers): full limits immediately.

## Transaction Alerts
- SMS and push notification sent for every outward transfer.
- If you receive an alert for a transfer you did not initiate: call 1800-NOVUS immediately to freeze account.
