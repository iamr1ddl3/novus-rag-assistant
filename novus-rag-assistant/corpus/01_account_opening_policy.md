# Account Opening Policy — Novus Bank

## Overview
Novus Bank offers fully digital account opening for Indian residents and NRIs. All accounts are opened via the Novus mobile app or website. Branch-based opening is available in select cities.

## Account Types

### Savings Account
- **Standard Tier:** Minimum Average Quarterly Balance (AQB) of ₹1,000. Non-maintenance penalty: ₹500/month.
- **Novus Plus Tier:** Zero balance account once Plus status is achieved (AQB ≥ ₹1 lakh maintained).
- **Novus Elite Tier:** Zero balance account; no penalty ever.
- Interest rate: 3.5% p.a. on daily balance up to ₹1 lakh; 4.0% above ₹1 lakh.

### Current Account
- Minimum balance: ₹10,000 (Standard), ₹5,000 (Plus), ₹0 (Elite).
- Overdraft facility available up to 2x monthly average balance for Plus/Elite customers.
- Business-verified accounts eligible for higher transaction limits.

### Joint Account
- Maximum 3 account holders.
- Operating mode: Either-or-Survivor (default) or Jointly (all signatures required).
- All holders must complete individual KYC.
- Nomination mandatory at account opening; up to 3 nominees with percentage allocation.

## KYC Requirements

### Mandatory Documents (All Customers)
1. **Aadhaar card** — linked to registered mobile number for OTP-based verification.
2. **PAN card** — mandatory for accounts with annual credits exceeding ₹2.5 lakh.

### Address Proof (one of the following)
- Aadhaar (if address is current)
- Passport
- Voter ID
- Utility bill (electricity/gas/telephone) — not older than 3 months
- Rent agreement with owner's address proof

### Video KYC
- Available 9 AM – 6 PM, Monday to Saturday (excluding public holidays).
- Requires Aadhaar OTP, PAN, and live selfie verification.
- Completion time: 10–15 minutes.
- Account activated within 2 hours of successful video KYC.

## Account Activation Timeline
- Video KYC: 2 hours post-approval.
- Document-based KYC: 1–2 working days.
- Account number issued immediately; full access after KYC approval.

## Eligibility
- Indian residents: 18 years and above. Minors (10–17) may open accounts with guardian as joint holder.
- NRIs: Additional documents required — valid passport, visa, overseas address proof. Must open NRE or NRO account type.
- Politically Exposed Persons (PEPs): Additional due diligence applies; processing time 5–7 working days.

## What Cannot Be Opened Online
- Corporate/business accounts (require branch visit and company documents).
- Accounts for non-individuals (trusts, HUFs, partnerships) — branch processing only.

## Account Number Format
- 12-digit account number assigned at activation.
- IFSC code: NOVS0001XXX (branch-specific suffix).

## Important Notes
- One savings account per individual PAN (exceptions for NRE/NRO combinations).
- Dormancy rules apply after 24 months of inactivity — see Account Closure and Dormancy Policy.
- Re-KYC required every 2 years — see KYC and Compliance Policy.
