# KYC and Compliance Policy — Novus Bank

## KYC Requirements for Account Opening

### Mandatory Documents
- **Identity Proof (any one):** Aadhaar card, Passport, Voter ID, Driving Licence.
- **Address Proof (any one):** Aadhaar card (if showing current address), Utility bill (not older than 3 months), Bank statement (other bank, not older than 3 months), Rental agreement.
- **PAN Card:** Mandatory for all accounts; Form 60 accepted only if PAN not yet allotted (temporary, must provide PAN within 6 months).
- **Photograph:** One recent passport-size photograph (digital upload accepted).

### Aadhaar + PAN — Most Common Combination
- Aadhaar provides both identity and address proof in a single document.
- PAN is mandatory for compliance with income tax regulations and PMLA.
- Aadhaar OTP-based e-KYC accepted for savings accounts (video KYC not required for standard accounts if e-KYC succeeds).

## Video KYC (V-KYC)

### When Required
- Account opening when Aadhaar e-KYC fails or is not available.
- Current accounts and business accounts.
- NRI/NRO accounts (in-branch or video KYC required; remote e-KYC not accepted).
- Re-KYC when customer flags an address change.

### Video KYC Process
1. Schedule via Novus app → Profile → KYC → Schedule Video KYC.
2. Available: Monday to Saturday, 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM IST.
3. **Original documents required** (not photocopies): Aadhaar + PAN + any address proof.
4. Hold documents clearly in front of camera; Novus agent will verify.
5. Live photo captured during session (no pre-recorded videos accepted).
6. Completion time: typically 10–15 minutes.

### Video KYC Rejection
- If rejected: customer receives email within 2 hours with specific reason.
- Customer has **30 days** to resubmit after a rejection.
- Reasons for rejection: document not legible, document expired, name mismatch, poor video quality.
- Three failed V-KYC attempts: branch visit mandatory.

## Periodic Re-KYC

### Re-KYC Schedule by Risk Category
| Customer Risk Category | Re-KYC Frequency |
|-----------------------|-----------------|
| Low risk (standard accounts) | Every **2 years** |
| Medium risk (high transaction accounts) | Every **1 year** |
| High risk (politically exposed persons, NRIs with large transactions) | Every **6 months** |

### Re-KYC Process
- Novus Bank sends reminder via app notification and registered email **60 days before** due date.
- Customer can complete re-KYC via: app (document upload), video KYC, or branch.
- **Grace period:** 30 days after due date. During grace period, account remains active.
- **After grace period:** Account marked restricted — only inward credits and standing instruction debits allowed; fresh outward transfers blocked.
- **After 90 days of non-compliance:** Account frozen pending regulatory action.

### Documents for Re-KYC
- If address unchanged and PAN already on file: Aadhaar OTP-based self-update may suffice (no new document upload).
- If address changed: New address proof mandatory.
- If PAN changed: New PAN card copy + declaration.

## AML / PMLA Compliance

### Transaction Monitoring
- Automated monitoring flags: cash deposits above ₹50,000/day, aggregate deposits above ₹10 lakh in a month, cross-border transfers above ₹1 lakh.
- Flagged transactions reviewed by Novus Bank compliance team within 2 working days.
- Customer may be asked to provide **Source of Funds (SoF)** declaration for large or unusual transactions.

### Source of Funds Declaration
- Required for: single cash deposit ≥ ₹2 lakh, cumulative deposits ≥ ₹10 lakh in 30 days (across all modes).
- Acceptable proofs: salary slip, investment redemption statement, property sale deed, gift deed from family member.
- **Failure to provide SoF within 7 days:** Account debits suspended pending compliance clearance.

### Suspicious Transaction Reporting
- Novus Bank is legally required to report suspicious transactions to FIU-IND (Financial Intelligence Unit — India).
- Customers will NOT be informed when a Suspicious Transaction Report (STR) is filed — this is legally prohibited.
- STR filing does not automatically freeze the account; freeze is a separate compliance action.

## PAN-Aadhaar Linking
- All accounts must have PAN linked to Aadhaar as per Government of India mandate.
- Customers with unlinked PAN-Aadhaar: TDS deducted at higher rate (20% instead of 10%).
- Novus Bank sends reminders but cannot link on customer's behalf — must be done via Income Tax portal.

## FATCA / CRS Declaration
- All account holders must declare tax residency status (Indian resident or foreign tax resident).
- Foreign nationals and NRIs must complete FATCA/CRS self-certification at account opening and update if residency changes.
- Non-declaration does not block account opening but triggers restricted status within 30 days.

## Document Retention
- KYC documents retained for minimum **10 years** after account closure per PMLA requirements.
- Customers can request a copy of KYC documents held by Novus Bank via a written request (processed within 15 working days).
