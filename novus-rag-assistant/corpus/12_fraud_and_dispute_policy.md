# Fraud and Dispute Policy — Novus Bank

## Reporting an Unauthorized Transaction
Report immediately via any of these channels:
1. **Novus app** → Transactions → Report Fraud (fastest)
2. **Helpline:** 1800-NOVUS (1800-66887) — toll-free, 24/7
3. **Email:** fraud@novusbank.in (include transaction details and screenshot)
4. **Branch visit** (for account compromise — bring photo ID)

**The sooner you report, the lower your liability.**

## Customer Liability by Reporting Window

| Reported Within | Customer Liability |
|----------------|-------------------|
| **3 calendar days** of the transaction | **Zero** (Novus Bank bears full loss) |
| 4–7 calendar days | Limited: up to ₹10,000 (customer bears) |
| More than 7 calendar days | Full transaction amount (customer bears) |

**Zero Liability Conditions (regardless of reporting window):**
- The fraud occurred due to deficiency in Novus Bank's system (not customer negligence).
- Card-present fraud (someone physically used your card without your knowledge).
- Phishing/vishing where Novus Bank's brand was impersonated.

**Full Liability Conditions (overrides zero-liability rule):**
- Customer shared OTP, PIN, or password voluntarily (even if deceived).
- Fraud resulted from gross negligence (e.g., writing PIN on card, sharing card details over phone).

## Immediate Steps After Reporting
1. Account/card blocked within 15 minutes of fraud report acknowledgment.
2. Reference number issued; note this for all follow-ups.
3. Provisional credit issued within **10 working days** for amounts above ₹10,000.
4. Formal investigation completed within **10 working days** (simple cases) to **45 working days** (complex cases involving multiple parties).

## Chargeback Process (Card Transactions)
- File chargeback within **30 days** of the transaction date (credit card statement date for credit cards).
- Evidence required: transaction receipt, communication with merchant, proof of non-delivery/defect.
- Chargeback raised with card network (Visa/Mastercard/RuPay) by Novus Bank on customer's behalf.
- Resolution timeline: 10–45 working days depending on merchant's response.

## Account Freeze
- Triggered automatically when: 3+ failed login attempts, fraud reported, unusual transaction pattern detected.
- During freeze: no outward transactions; inward credits allowed.
- Unfreeze: after successful identity verification at branch or via video KYC call.
- If account is frozen due to regulatory/legal order, unfreeze requires compliance team clearance.

## Investigation Process
1. Novus Bank forensics team reviews transaction logs, device fingerprints, and IP addresses.
2. If fraud confirmed: full amount credited back permanently; police complaint filed by Novus Bank.
3. If fraud not confirmed (e.g., merchant dispute resolved in merchant's favor): provisional credit reversed with 5-day notice.
4. Customer can escalate to L2 Grievance if dissatisfied with investigation outcome.

## SIM Swap Fraud
- If you suspect your SIM has been swapped (loss of mobile signal + unauthorized transactions): report to Novus Bank AND your telecom operator immediately.
- Novus Bank will block all OTP-based authentication within 30 minutes of report.
- Account access restored only after in-person verification at Novus branch.

## Common Fraud Scenarios to Watch For
- **UPI collect requests:** Never pay to "receive" money. Legitimate refunds do not require your PIN.
- **Fake customer care:** Novus Bank's only helpline is 1800-NOVUS. Do not call numbers from Google search ads.
- **Screen sharing scams:** Novus Bank agents never ask you to install remote access apps.
- **KYC expiry scams:** Novus Bank sends KYC notices only via registered email/app; never via WhatsApp.
