# Investment Products — Novus Bank

## Overview
Novus Bank offers investment products in partnership with SEBI-registered entities. Investments are accessible via the Novus app under the "Invest" tab. Novus Bank acts as a distributor, not an advisor; customers are encouraged to consult a financial advisor.

## Risk Profiling (Mandatory)
- All customers must complete a risk profile assessment before their first investment.
- Assessment is a 10-question questionnaire covering investment goals, risk tolerance, and time horizon.
- Risk score: 1 (Conservative) to 5 (Aggressive).
- Profile valid for 2 years; updated automatically if significant account activity changes are detected.
- Cannot be skipped; prevents unsuitable product recommendations.

## Mutual Funds

### Onboarding
- KYC linked to Novus Bank account (no separate MF KYC required if bank KYC is complete).
- Access to 1,000+ mutual fund schemes across all AMFI-registered AMCs.
- Direct plans only (no distributor commission; higher returns than regular plans).

### SIP (Systematic Investment Plan)
- Minimum SIP amount: **₹500/month**.
- Maximum active SIPs simultaneously: **20**.
- SIP dates available: 1st, 5th, 10th, 15th, 20th, 25th of each month.
- Auto-debit via UPI mandate from Novus savings account.
- Pause SIP: up to 3 months without cancellation (via app).
- Stop/cancel SIP: effective from next SIP date if cancelled at least 2 working days in advance.

### Lump-Sum Investment
- Minimum: ₹1,000.
- Payment via savings account balance (instant) or UPI (T+1 for NAV application).
- NAV applied based on BSE/NSE submission time: before 3 PM → same-day NAV; after 3 PM → next day NAV.

### Redemption
- Equity funds: proceeds credited to savings account within **T+3 working days**.
- Debt funds: proceeds within **T+2 working days**.
- Liquid/overnight funds: proceeds same day if redeemed before 12 PM.
- Exit load: varies by fund; shown prominently before investment confirmation.

## Digital Gold

### Details
- 999.9 purity (24-carat), stored in SEBI-compliant insured vaults (SafeGold/MMTC-PAMP).
- Minimum investment: **₹100**.
- No maximum limit.
- Price linked to live MCX gold rates + 1% spread.
- GST applicable at 3% on purchase; not applicable on redemption.

### Physical Delivery
- Convert to physical gold at ≥ 1 gram (minimum).
- Delivery: 7–10 working days; available in 1g, 2g, 5g, 10g, and 50g coin denominations.
- Delivery charge: ₹99 (1g–5g), ₹199 (10g+).

## National Pension System (NPS)

### Tier 1 (Retirement Account)
- Mandatory for NPS enrollment; locked till age 60.
- Minimum annual contribution: ₹1,000.
- Tax benefit: ₹1,50,000 under Section 80C + additional ₹50,000 under Section 80CCD(1B).
- Partial withdrawal: allowed after 3 years for specific purposes (higher education, medical emergency, home purchase).
- At 60: minimum 40% must be used to buy annuity; remaining 60% is tax-free lump sum.

### Tier 2 (Voluntary Savings)
- Open only if Tier 1 is active.
- No lock-in; withdraw anytime.
- No additional tax benefit (except for government employees under Section 80C).
- Minimum contribution: ₹250 per transaction.

## Sovereign Gold Bonds (SGBs)
- Government of India bonds denominated in grams of gold.
- Available during RBI-announced subscription windows (typically 6–8 windows/year).
- Interest: 2.5% p.a. on initial investment (paid semi-annually, fully taxable).
- Capital gains on redemption at maturity (8 years) are tax-exempt.
- Minimum: 1 gram; Maximum: 4 kg/financial year for individuals.

## General Disclaimers
- Mutual fund investments are subject to market risk. Past performance is not indicative of future returns.
- Digital gold prices fluctuate with market conditions.
- NPS returns are market-linked; Novus Bank does not guarantee returns on any investment product.
