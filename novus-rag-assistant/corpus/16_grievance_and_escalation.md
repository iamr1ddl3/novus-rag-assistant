# Grievance Redressal and Escalation Policy — Novus Bank

## Overview
Novus Bank has a structured 3-level grievance redressal framework compliant with RBI guidelines. All complaints are tracked with a unique reference number and must be resolved within defined timelines.

## Level 1 (L1) — First-Point Resolution

### Channels
- **Novus App:** Help → Raise a Complaint (fastest; auto-assigns ticket and tracks status).
- **Helpline:** 1800-NOVUS (1800-66887), available 24/7 for urgent issues; 8:00 AM – 8:00 PM for non-urgent.
- **Email:** support@novusbank.in
- **Branch:** Relationship Manager or Branch Manager.
- **Chatbot (Novus Assist):** Handles common queries; auto-escalates unresolved conversations to human agent.

### Resolution Timeline
- **Acknowledgement:** Within **4 hours** of complaint submission.
- **Resolution:** Within **3 working days** for standard complaints.
- **Priority complaints** (fraud, account freeze, failed EMI): Within **1 working day**.

### What L1 Handles
- Transaction queries (failed payments, reversals, incorrect charges).
- Card issues (blocking, replacement requests).
- Account statement requests.
- Basic KYC update queries.
- Loan EMI queries (not disputes).
- General product information.

### L1 Reference Number
- Every complaint receives a ticket ID (format: NVS-YYYY-XXXXXXXX).
- Retain this ID for all follow-ups; status trackable via app.

---

## Level 2 (L2) — Grievance Redressal Officer

### When to Escalate to L2
- L1 resolution not received within 3 working days.
- Customer dissatisfied with L1 resolution.
- Complaints involving: loan restructuring decisions, fraud investigation outcomes, account closure disputes, TDS certificate errors.

### How to Escalate
- App: Help → My Complaints → [Ticket ID] → Escalate to L2.
- Email: grievance@novusbank.in (include L1 ticket ID in subject line).
- Written letter to Grievance Redressal Officer, Novus Bank Head Office, Bengaluru.

### Resolution Timeline
- **Acknowledgement:** Within **24 hours**.
- **Resolution:** Within **7 working days**.
- L2 Grievance Officer issues a written response (email) with resolution and reasoning.

### L2 Compensation Policy
- If L2 upholds the complaint and delay was on Novus Bank's part: customer is eligible for **compensation of ₹100 per day** of delay beyond the L1 resolution SLA.
- Maximum compensation per complaint: ₹5,000.
- Compensation credited directly to account; no separate claim needed.

---

## Level 3 (L3) — Internal Ombudsman

### When to Escalate to L3
- L2 resolution not received within 7 working days, OR
- Customer dissatisfied with L2 response.

### How to Escalate
- Email: internal.ombudsman@novusbank.in
- Only available after exhausting L2 (L3 will reject complaints that skipped L2).

### Resolution Timeline
- **Acknowledgement:** Within **24 hours**.
- **Final decision:** Within **15 working days**.
- L3 decision is binding on Novus Bank (but not on the customer — customer retains right to approach RBI).

---

## RBI Banking Ombudsman (External)

### Eligibility
Customer can approach RBI Banking Ombudsman if:
- Novus Bank has not replied within **30 days** of complaint, OR
- Customer is dissatisfied with Novus Bank's final response (L3 or below).

### Filing a Complaint
- Portal: **cms.rbi.org.in** (RBI Complaint Management System).
- No fee for filing with RBI Ombudsman.
- Complaints must be filed within **1 year** of the date of bank's final reply (or 1 year + 1 month if no reply received).
- RBI Ombudsman can award compensation up to ₹20 lakh for losses, plus ₹1 lakh for harassment/mental agony.

### Complaints NOT Accepted by RBI Ombudsman
- Matters already decided by a court.
- Complaints older than 1 year (from bank's final reply).
- Complaints related to products not regulated by RBI (mutual funds, insurance — handled by SEBI/IRDAI ombudsman).

|----------|--------|--------|
| Failed transaction / reversal | 1 working day | 3 working days |
| Unauthorized transaction / fraud | 1 working day | 5 working days |
| Account freeze / unfreeze | 1 working day | 3 working days |
| Loan EMI dispute | 3 working days | 7 working days |
| KYC-related | 3 working days | 7 working days |
| Card dispute / chargeback | 3 working days | 7 working days |
| Fee/charge reversal | 3 working days | 5 working days |

## Complaint Withdrawal
- Customer can withdraw a complaint at any time before final resolution.
- Withdrawn complaints cannot be re-raised for the same issue unless new evidence emerges.
- Withdrawal is irreversible once confirmed.

## Feedback and CSAT
- After every complaint resolution, Novus Bank sends a CSAT survey (1–5 stars).
- Rating ≤ 2 automatically triggers an L2 follow-up call within 2 working days.
- CSAT data is reviewed monthly by the Quality Assurance team.
