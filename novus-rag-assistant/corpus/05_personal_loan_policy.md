# Personal Loan Policy — Novus Bank

## Overview
Novus Bank offers instant digital personal loans for salaried and self-employed individuals. Loan disbursal happens within 4 hours for pre-approved customers and within 2 working days for new applicants.

## Eligibility Criteria

| Criterion | Salaried | Self-Employed |
|-----------|----------|---------------|
| Minimum age | 21 years | 25 years |
| Maximum age at loan maturity | 60 years | 65 years |
| Minimum monthly income | ₹25,000 | ₹4,00,000/year (net profit) |
| Minimum CIBIL score | 700 | 720 |
| Employment stability | 1 year with current employer | 2 years in current business |
| Novus Bank account | Preferred but not mandatory | Mandatory for self-employed |

## Loan Parameters

- **Loan amount:** ₹50,000 to ₹25,00,000.
- **Tenure:** 12 to 60 months.
- **Interest rate (reducing balance):**
  - Elite customers: 11.00% – 13.00% p.a.
  - Plus customers: 13.00% – 15.00% p.a.
  - Standard customers: 15.00% – 18.00% p.a.
  - Final rate determined by CIBIL score, income, and loan amount.

## Fees and Charges

| Charge | Amount |
|--------|--------|
| Processing fee | 1% of loan amount (minimum ₹1,000, maximum ₹15,000) |
| Documentation fee | ₹500 (waived for Elite customers) |
| Prepayment fee | 2% of outstanding principal (applicable after 6 EMIs) |
| Foreclosure fee | 1% of outstanding principal (applicable after 12 EMIs) |
| EMI bounce charge | ₹500 per bounce |
| Late payment interest | 2% per month on overdue EMI amount |
| Loan cancellation fee | ₹2,000 (before first disbursement) |

## Pre-Payment Terms
- **Partial prepayment:** Minimum ₹10,000 per transaction. Allowed after 6 EMIs.
- **Full foreclosure:** Allowed after 12 EMIs. Penalty: 1% on outstanding principal + GST.
- **No-penalty foreclosure:** Available for Elite customers after 18 EMIs.

## Required Documents
- **Salaried:** Last 3 months' salary slips, last 6 months' bank statement, Aadhaar, PAN, employer ID.
- **Self-employed:** Last 2 years' ITR with computation, CA-certified P&L and balance sheet, last 6 months' business bank statement, GST registration (if applicable), Aadhaar, PAN.

## Disbursement
- Pre-approved offer customers (existing Novus accounts): disbursement within 4 hours of e-signing loan agreement.
- New applicants: 1–2 working days after document verification.
- Amount credited directly to Novus Bank savings account (or NEFT to other bank for non-Novus customers).

## EMI Deduction
- Auto-debit set up via NACH mandate.
- EMI date choices: 5th, 10th, or 15th of each month.
- If account has insufficient balance on EMI date, retry on the next 2 working days before marking as bounce.

## Loan Statement and NOC
- Statement: downloadable from app anytime.
- NOC (No Objection Certificate): issued within 7 working days of loan closure. Physical copy dispatched; digital copy available in app.

## Multiple Loans
- Maximum 2 active personal loans simultaneously.
- Total EMI obligation across all loans (including home and auto loans) should not exceed 50% of net monthly income at time of application.
