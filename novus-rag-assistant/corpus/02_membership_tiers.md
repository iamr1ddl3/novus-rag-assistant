# Membership Tiers — Novus Bank

## Overview
Novus Bank offers three membership tiers: Standard, Novus Plus, and Novus Elite. Tier status is evaluated on the first business day of each calendar quarter based on the previous quarter's Average Quarterly Balance (AQB) across all savings and fixed deposit accounts.

## Tier Definitions

### Standard (Default)
- All new customers start at Standard.
- No minimum balance requirement beyond savings account policy.
- Full access to digital banking features.

### Novus Plus
- **Eligibility:** Maintain AQB of ₹1,00,000 (₹1 lakh) or more across savings + FD accounts for the full quarter.
- **Upgrade:** Automatic at next quarter review if AQB criterion is met for 2 consecutive quarters.
- **Downgrade:** If AQB drops below ₹75,000 (25% grace threshold) for 2 consecutive quarters, reverted to Standard with 30-day notice.

### Novus Elite
- **Eligibility:** Maintain AQB of ₹5,00,000 (₹5 lakh) or more, OR maintain investments (mutual funds + NPS + digital gold) of ₹10,00,000 or more.
- **Upgrade:** Automatic at next quarter review. Existing Plus members fast-tracked if threshold crossed mid-quarter.
- **Downgrade:** If AQB drops below ₹3,75,000 (25% grace) AND investments below ₹7,50,000 for 2 consecutive quarters.

## Relationship Manager (Elite Only)
- Assigned within 5 working days of Elite upgrade.
- Dedicated mobile number and email; available 9 AM – 7 PM Monday to Saturday.
- Handles: investment advisory, loan applications, complaint escalation, account services.
- If RM is unavailable, any Elite support agent can access the RM's account notes and continue service.

## Tier Review Schedule
- Quarterly: April 1, July 1, October 1, January 1.
- Customers receive SMS/email 30 days before potential downgrade.
- Tier changes take effect the first working day of the new quarter.

## Lounge Access (Elite)
- Valid at all partner domestic airport lounges (85+ airports).
- 4 complimentary visits per calendar year per primary account holder.
- Additional visits: ₹500 per visit charged to linked account.
- Companion access: available at ₹500/visit (not counted against free quota).
- Access via Novus Elite Debit Card tap at lounge entry; no pre-booking required.

## Retention Offers
- Elite customers at risk of downgrade may be eligible for retention offers (subject to discretion) — see Internal Agent Guidelines.
