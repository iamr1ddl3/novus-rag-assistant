# Home Loan Policy — Novus Bank

## Overview
Novus Bank offers home loans for purchase, construction, renovation, and balance transfer. All home loan applications are processed digitally with property verification by Novus-empanelled valuers.

## Loan-to-Value (LTV) Ratios

| Loan Amount | Maximum LTV |
|-------------|-------------|
| Up to ₹30 lakh | 80% of property value |
| ₹30 lakh – ₹75 lakh | 75% of property value |
| Above ₹75 lakh | 70% of property value |

LTV is calculated on registered market value or Novus Bank's valuation, whichever is lower.

## Interest Rates
- **Floating rate:** Repo Rate + 2.50% spread (currently 8.50% p.a. as of this policy version). Reset every quarter.
- **Fixed rate (5-year lock-in):** 9.00% p.a. Fixed for first 5 years, then converts to floating.
- Rate of interest communicated in the loan sanction letter; final rate may differ from indicative rate based on credit assessment.

## Eligibility

| Criterion | Salaried | Self-Employed |
|-----------|----------|---------------|
| Minimum age | 21 years | 25 years |
| Maximum age at loan maturity | 70 years | 70 years |
| Minimum income | ₹30,000/month | ₹5,00,000/year |
| CIBIL score | ≥ 700 | ≥ 720 |
| Employer stability | 2 years | 3 years in business |

## Tenure
- Minimum: 5 years.
- Maximum: 30 years (subject to borrower not exceeding age 70 at maturity).

## Co-Applicant Rules
- Co-applicant is mandatory if sole borrower is not the sole property owner.
- Eligible co-applicants: spouse, parents, siblings (unmarried and financially dependent), adult children.
- Co-applicant income may be clubbed to enhance loan eligibility.
- Co-applicant must be KYC-compliant Novus Bank account holder.

## Pre-Payment Terms
- **Floating rate loans:** Zero pre-payment penalty at any time.
- **Fixed rate loans:** 2% penalty on amount prepaid during the 5-year lock-in period. Zero penalty after lock-in.
- Minimum partial prepayment: ₹1,00,000.
- Pre-payment via NEFT/RTGS to designated Novus Home Loans account; do not use UPI.

## Balance Transfer
- External home loan can be transferred to Novus Bank.
- Eligibility: minimum 12 EMIs paid at existing lender, no default in last 12 months.
- Processing fee: 0.5% of outstanding principal (waived for Elite customers).
- Top-up loan available up to 20% of outstanding principal at balance transfer.

## Required Documents
- Aadhaar, PAN, last 3 months' salary slips (salaried) or 2 years' ITR (self-employed).
- Last 12 months' bank statement.
- Property documents: sale agreement / allotment letter / title deed, property tax receipt, approved building plan.
- NOC from housing society (if applicable).

## Disbursement
- Disbursed directly to seller/builder after legal and technical clearance.
- For construction: tranche-based disbursement linked to construction milestones verified by Novus valuer.

## Property Insurance
- Home loan customers are required to insure the property for at least the loan outstanding amount.
- Novus Bank-partnered insurance products available; customer may choose any IRDAI-registered insurer.

## Foreclosure
- Allowed after 12 EMIs (floating) or after 5-year lock-in (fixed).
- Foreclosure certificate and original property documents returned within 15 working days of final payment.
