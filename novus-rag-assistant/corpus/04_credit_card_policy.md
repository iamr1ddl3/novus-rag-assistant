# Credit Card Policy — Novus Bank

## Card Variants

### Novus Silver Credit Card
- **Eligibility:** Standard/Plus tier, CIBIL score ≥ 700, minimum income ₹25,000/month (salaried) or ₹4 lakh/year (self-employed).
- **Credit limit:** ₹25,000 – ₹1,00,000 (set at issuance based on CIBIL and income).
- **Rewards:** 2% cashback on groceries and utility bills; 1% on all other spends.
- **Annual fee:** ₹999 (waived if annual spends exceed ₹1,20,000).

### Novus Gold Credit Card
- **Eligibility:** Plus tier, CIBIL ≥ 730, minimum income ₹50,000/month.
- **Credit limit:** ₹1,00,000 – ₹5,00,000.
- **Rewards:** 3% cashback on dining and food delivery; 2% on travel and fuel; 1% on all others.
- **Annual fee:** ₹2,499 (waived if annual spends exceed ₹3,00,000).
- **Fuel surcharge waiver:** 1% surcharge waived at all fuel stations, up to ₹250/month.

### Novus Platinum Credit Card
- **Eligibility:** Elite tier only, CIBIL ≥ 750.
- **Credit limit:** ₹5,00,000 – ₹25,00,000.
- **Rewards:** 5% cashback on all categories (maximum ₹5,000/month cashback cap).
- **Annual fee:** ₹9,999 (waived if annual spends exceed ₹12,00,000).
- **Additional perks:** 8 domestic lounge visits/year, 2 international lounge visits/year, concierge service, zero forex markup.

## Billing Cycle
- **Statement close date:** 15th of every month.
- **Payment due date:** 5th of the following month (20-day interest-free period).
- Statements sent via email and app notification on the 16th.
- If the 5th falls on a public holiday, payment due on the next working day.

## Payment Options
- **Full payment:** No interest charged.
- **Minimum payment:** 5% of total outstanding OR ₹500, whichever is higher.
- **Any amount in between:** Interest charged on the unpaid balance from transaction date.

## Interest and Charges

| Charge | Silver | Gold | Platinum |
|--------|--------|------|----------|
| Revolving interest rate | 3.5%/month (42% p.a.) | 3.5%/month | 3.0%/month (36% p.a.) |
| Late payment fee | ₹500 | ₹750 | ₹1,000 |
| Over-limit fee | ₹500 | ₹750 | ₹1,000 |
| Cash advance fee | 2.5% (min ₹500) | 2.5% | 2.0% |
| Cash advance interest | 3.5%/month from Day 1 | 3.5%/month | 3.0%/month |
| Cheque bounce fee | ₹500 | ₹500 | ₹500 |

**Important:** Cash advances do not have an interest-free period. Interest starts accruing from the transaction date.

## Credit Limit Management
- **Increase request:** Eligible after 6 months of card use with consistent on-time payments; apply via app.
- **Temporary increase:** Available for up to 30 days for large purchases; apply at least 3 working days in advance.
- **Decrease:** Effective immediately on request via app.
- **Emergency block:** Blocks all new transactions while keeping existing ones for repayment.

## EMI Conversion
- Convert purchases of ₹2,000 or more into EMI post-transaction (within 30 days).
- Available tenures: 3, 6, 9, 12 months.
- EMI interest rate: 14–18% p.a. depending on tenure and card type.
- Processing fee: 1% of EMI amount (min ₹99).
- Pre-closure allowed after 3 EMIs; 2% pre-closure charge on outstanding principal.

## Reward Points
- Points credited within 2 working days of transaction.
- Redemption: Statement credit, Amazon/Flipkart vouchers, travel bookings.
- Validity: 3 years from date of earn; Silver/Gold points expire on card closure.
- Platinum: points never expire as long as card is active.

## Card Closure
- Outstanding balance must be cleared before closure.
- Reward points can be redeemed up to 30 days after closure request.
- Closure confirmation sent via registered email within 7 working days.
