# UPI and Digital Payments Policy — Novus Bank

## UPI Setup
- UPI ID format: `mobilenumber@novus` or customer-chosen handle `@novus`.
- Up to 3 UPI IDs per Novus Bank account.
- Link up to 5 external bank accounts to one Novus Pay UPI ID.
- Set up via Novus app, Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM, or any NPCI-approved TPAP.
- UPI PIN: 4 or 6 digits, set by customer; never stored by Novus Bank.

## Transaction Limits (UPI)

| Limit Type | Value |
|------------|-------|
| Per transaction | ₹1,00,000 |
| Per day (all UPI transactions combined) | ₹1,00,000 |
| Per week | ₹5,00,000 |
| Merchant QR code payment (no PIN required) | Up to ₹2,000 |

- Special higher limits (up to ₹5,00,000 per transaction) available for: hospital payments, education fees, IPO applications, tax payments. Applied automatically based on merchant category.

## Failed Transactions and Reversals
- **Money deducted but not credited to beneficiary:** Auto-reversal within **24 hours** in most cases; maximum 3 working days as per NPCI guidelines.
- **Pending (grey) status:** Transaction in process; do not retry. If not resolved in 1 hour, contact support.
- **Declined at your bank:** No deduction occurred; retry immediately or try a different payment method.
- **Declined at beneficiary bank:** Amount not deducted; retry after 30 minutes.

## Raising a UPI Dispute
- File dispute via: Novus app → UPI → Transaction History → Raise Dispute.
- Dispute window: **within 3 business days** of the transaction date for auto-escalation to NPCI.
- After 3 days: dispute still accepted but resolution may take up to 30 days.
- For ₹10,000+ disputed amounts, provisional credit may be issued within 5 working days pending investigation.

## UPI PIN Security
- **3 wrong UPI PIN attempts:** UPI blocked for 24 hours. Reset via app using debit card details.
- Never share UPI PIN with anyone, including Novus Bank representatives.
- Novus Bank will never call and ask for UPI PIN.
- Collect requests (where someone requests money from you) never require PIN entry — accepting sends money TO them.

## Novus Pay App Features
- **Schedule payments:** Set future-dated UPI payments up to 30 days in advance.
- **Split bills:** Split with up to 20 contacts from phone book.
- **Payment links:** Generate and share via WhatsApp/SMS.
- **Recurring payments:** Auto-pay mandates for subscriptions (Netflix, Jio, etc.) — set once, paid automatically.
- **Transaction history:** Searchable 12-month history with category tags.

## UPI Autopay (Recurring Mandates)
- Customers can approve UPI AutoPay mandates for recurring charges.
- Pre-debit notification sent 24 hours before each debit.
- Maximum per-transaction limit for autopay: ₹15,000 (standard); ₹1,00,000 for utility/insurance.
- Pause, modify, or revoke any mandate via app under UPI AutoPay section.

## Bharat BillPay (BBPS)
- Pay electricity, water, gas, broadband, DTH, insurance premiums via Novus app.
- Payment reflects with biller within 24–48 hours.
- Confirmation receipt downloadable for all BBPS payments.

## Common Issues and Solutions

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|---------|
| "Bank server down" error | Retry after 30 minutes; typically resolved within 2 hours |
| "Transaction limit exceeded" | Wait till midnight for daily limit reset |
| "Invalid UPI ID" | Verify spelling; ask beneficiary to confirm their UPI ID |
| Duplicate charge visible | Check if it's a pending reversal; wait 24 hours before raising dispute |
