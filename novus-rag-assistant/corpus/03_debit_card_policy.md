# Debit Card Policy — Novus Bank

## Card Variants
- **Standard Debit Card:** Visa/RuPay Classic. Issued free with all savings accounts.
- **Plus Debit Card:** Visa Gold. Auto-upgraded on Plus tier activation.
- **Elite Debit Card:** Visa Platinum. Auto-upgraded on Elite tier activation; metal card option available (₹999 one-time).

## Transaction Limits (Daily)

| Transaction Type | Standard | Novus Plus | Novus Elite |
|-----------------|----------|------------|-------------|
| ATM cash withdrawal | ₹50,000 | ₹1,00,000 | ₹2,00,000 |
| POS / e-commerce | ₹2,00,000 | ₹5,00,000 | ₹10,00,000 |
| Contactless (tap-to-pay) | ₹5,000 per tap (no PIN) | ₹5,000 per tap | ₹5,000 per tap |
| International ATM | Disabled by default | ₹50,000 | ₹1,50,000 |
| International POS | Disabled by default | ₹2,00,000 | ₹5,00,000 |

Limits reset at midnight IST daily. Customers can reduce limits permanently or temporarily via the app.

## ATM Usage
- **Free transactions per month:**
  - Novus ATMs: 5 transactions free (all tiers).
  - Other bank ATMs: Standard — 3 free; Plus — 5 free; Elite — unlimited free.
  - After free quota: ₹20 per transaction + GST.
- **Cash withdrawal abroad:** 3.5% of amount + ₹150 flat fee (Standard/Plus). Elite: 1.5% only.
- Balance inquiry at non-Novus ATMs: ₹8 per inquiry after free quota.

## Contactless Payments
- Enabled by default on all cards.
- Transactions up to ₹5,000 do not require PIN entry.
- Transactions above ₹5,000 require PIN regardless of channel.
- Disable contactless via app settings or by calling support.

## International Usage
- **Default state:** International transactions disabled for security.
- **Enable temporarily:** Via app — choose duration: 1 day, 7 days, 30 days, 60 days, or 90 days.
- **Enable permanently:** Call 1800-NOVUS (toll-free) with verbal OTP confirmation.
- International transactions trigger real-time SMS/push notification.

## Card Blocking and Hotlisting
- **Temporary block:** Via app → Cards → Block Card. Re-enable anytime.
- **Permanent hotlisting:** Via app, 1800-NOVUS helpline, or any Novus branch.
- Replacement card dispatched within 24 hours of hotlisting.
- **Delivery time:** Standard 7–10 working days; Plus 3–5 working days; Elite 1–2 working days (courier).
- **Expedited same-city delivery:** Available for Elite at ₹299.

## PIN Management
- PIN set during card activation via app (no PIN mailed).
- Change PIN: app, Novus ATM, or phone banking.
- **Wrong PIN lockout:** 3 consecutive wrong PINs → card blocked for 24 hours (ATM channel) or 4 hours (POS channel). Reset via app or helpline.
- PIN never stored by Novus Bank; cannot be retrieved, only reset.

## Card Replacement Fees
- Lost/stolen: ₹150 (Standard), ₹100 (Plus), Free (Elite).
- Damaged: ₹150 (Standard), ₹100 (Plus), Free (Elite).
- Requested upgrade (e.g., Visa to RuPay): ₹150 (Standard); not applicable for tier-based upgrades.

## Fraud Liability
- Transactions made after reporting card lost/stolen: zero customer liability.
- Transactions before reporting: refer to Fraud and Dispute Policy.
- For disputes on card transactions, file within 30 days of the statement date.
