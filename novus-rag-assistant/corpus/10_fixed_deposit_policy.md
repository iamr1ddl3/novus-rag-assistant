# Fixed Deposit Policy — Novus Bank

## Overview
Novus Bank offers Fixed Deposits (FDs) with tenures from 7 days to 10 years. FDs can be opened via app, net banking, or branch. Interest is compounded quarterly for tenures of 6 months or more; simple interest for tenures below 6 months.

## Interest Rates (Standard Customers)

| Tenure | Interest Rate (p.a.) |
|--------|---------------------|
| 7 days – 29 days | 3.50% |
| 30 days – 90 days | 4.50% |
| 91 days – 180 days | 5.50% |
| 181 days – 364 days | 6.50% |
| 1 year – 2 years | 7.20% |
| 2 years – 5 years | 7.50% |
| 5 years – 10 years | 7.80% |

**Tier Bonuses:**
- **Novus Plus customers:** +0.50% p.a. on all tenures.
- **Novus Elite customers:** +1.00% p.a. on all tenures.
- **Senior citizens (60+ years):** Additional +0.50% p.a. on all tenures (stackable with tier bonus).

Example: Elite senior citizen, 3-year FD → 7.50% + 1.00% + 0.50% = **9.00% p.a.**

## Opening an FD
- Minimum deposit: ₹1,000.
- Maximum: No limit (TDS rules apply above ₹40,000 annual interest).
- Multiple FDs allowed simultaneously.
- Nomination: strongly recommended; can be the same as savings account nominee.

## Interest Payout Options
1. **Cumulative (default):** Interest compounded quarterly and paid at maturity. Best for wealth building.
2. **Non-cumulative:** Interest paid monthly, quarterly, half-yearly, or annually. Suitable for regular income.
3. For non-cumulative FDs below 6 months: interest paid at maturity only (simple interest).

## TDS (Tax Deducted at Source)
- TDS deducted at **10%** if total interest across all Novus FDs exceeds **₹40,000/financial year** (₹50,000 for senior citizens).
- Submit Form 15G (individuals below 60) or 15H (senior citizens) by April 30 each year to avoid TDS if total income is below taxable threshold.
- TDS certificate (Form 16A) issued quarterly.

## Premature Withdrawal
- Allowed at any time after 7 days from FD opening date.
- **Penalty:** 1% deducted from the applicable rate for the period the FD was held.
- Example: FD booked at 7.20% for 2 years, broken at 14 months → applicable rate for 14 months is 7.20% (as booked rate applies for completed tenure), minus 1% penalty = 6.20% effective rate.
- FDs used as loan collateral cannot be prematurely withdrawn while loan is outstanding.

## Auto-Renewal
- **Default:** FDs auto-renew at the prevailing rate for the same tenure on maturity date.
- Change renewal instruction via app up to 2 days before maturity.
- Options: renew same tenure, renew different tenure, pay out to savings account.
- If app instruction is missed: FD will auto-renew; instruction can still be changed within 5 working days of auto-renewal without penalty.

## Loan Against FD
- Overdraft up to **90% of FD value** at interest rate of FD rate + 1.5%.
- Minimum FD tenure of 6 months required.
- Loan does not break the FD; FD continues earning interest.
- Available as demand loan or overdraft (revolving credit).

## Tax-Saving FD (Section 80C)
- Tenure: exactly **5 years** (no premature withdrawal allowed).
- Deduction up to ₹1,50,000 per financial year under Section 80C.
- Not eligible for loan against FD.
- Interest is fully taxable; only principal deposit is eligible for 80C deduction.
